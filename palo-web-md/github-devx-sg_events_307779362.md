## Details

**This launch event, powered by PALO IT and sponsored by GitHub is all about energy, exploration, and getting connected.** Expect live demos straight from Build 2025, real developer stories, community vibes, and games to top it off. Let’s celebrate what it means to be a developer in this AI-powered era—together.

**6:00 PM – 6:30 PM - Registration & Networking**

* Check-in
* Pick up your exclusive GitHub swag
* Casual mingling over welcome drinks

**6:30 PM – 7:00 PM - Pizza, Snacks and Drinks**

* Enjoy food and drinks while making new connections across the tech community
* **7:00 PM – 7:10 PM - Opening Remarks by Annie Matthews, Director of Developer Productivity, Microsoft**
* Kick-off by GitHub team and community leads
* What DevEx means and why it matters now more than ever

**7:10 PM – 7:30 PM - Talk #1: PALO IT x GitHub – AI in Action by Kevin Aubry CTO**

*From Code to Impact: How Gen-E2 & GitHub Accelerate Innovation*

PALO IT shares their journey building Gen-E2, our trademarked AI-enabled PDLC methodology. Learn how their partnership with GitHub empowers faster iteration, seamless DevSecOps, and developer happiness through meaningful automation.

**7:30 PM – 8:15 PM - GitHub Demos -** *The Developer’s AI Toolkit: What’s New from Build* **by Arnaud Lheureux**

Experience the future of developer productivity with GitHub’s latest AI-powered tools and integrations. From enterprise-ready Copilot extensions to new automations that streamline your CI/CD workflows, this demo brings Build's biggest announcements to life—interactive, practical, and built for devs.

**8:15 PM onwards - Games & Networking: “You Miss 100% of the Shots You Don’t Take”**

* Wind down with a social round of digitized darts—fun for pros and first-timers alike
* Keep the energy up with drinks, good conversations, and playful competition
