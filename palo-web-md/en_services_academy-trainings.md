### Streamlining digital delivery through Scrum

### Challenge:

AXA initiated its digital transformation by adopting Scrum as a delivery framework for its e-commerce platform. The organization needed to quickly ramp up its delivery capabilities, making it crucial to onboard the team effectively with the Scrum delivery cadence.

### Solution:

PALO IT clarified the product backlog and established an agreed sprint cadence, creating a regular rhythm for the team’s delivery process for new products. Our team also provided technical training on design options and application supportability, while engaging other parts of AXA to bridge silos.

### AI for everyone: unlocking Pernod Ricard's potential

### Challenge:

Pernod Ricard Mexico aimed to leverage AI while ensuring employee engagement. The goal was to train nearly 400 staff—ranging from leaders to non-technical roles—in AI applications to drive value across the organization.

### Solution:

PALO IT implemented an AI Academy that offered training and practical workshops designed for non-tech profiles. The program included sessions using tools like ChatGPT and encouraged participants to lead pilot projects.
