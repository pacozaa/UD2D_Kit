## Ensure your products succeed with the right commercial model

We understand the challenges organizations face when bringing products to market—struggling with ineffective commercial models, low ROI, and missed opportunities for growth. Our discovery workshop services center on developing a results-driven business strategy that unlocks value through the effective use of data.

By analyzing existing processes and systems, we identify inefficiencies and recommend solutions (think AI automation tools and advanced data analytics) to drive operational efficiency, cost reduction, and create new business value that impacts the triple bottom line: profit, people, planet.

### Transforming a business model for sustainable excellence

### Challenge:

Concept 4, a global sourcing company, faced growing demands for ESG transparency. This required a fundamental transformation of their business model to incorporate automated carbon accounting processes.

### Solution:

PALO IT partnered with them to undergo a sustainable business model transformation, by developing an ESG data platform to automate carbon accounting and provide a 360° view of performance.

### 5.6K tons

Of actionable carbon reduction identified: Clearing a path for significant carbon reductions within Concept 4's operations and supply chain.

### 350+

Supplier contracts transformed: Improving supplier relationships, and enabling better alignment with sustainability goals.

### 2,500+

Products forecasted in carbon emissions: Providing clients with detailed insights into emissions related to product lifecycle management.

### Sustainable living plan

### Sustainable living plan

### Challenge:

Unilever sought to laser-focus on the environmental and social impact of its products while maintaining profitability and market growth, all amidst rising consumer expectations for sustainability.

### Solution:

The company put its ‘Sustainable Living Plan’ into action, working sustainability into its business strategy and focusing on responsible sourcing, reducing waste, and improving health.

### 69%

Faster growth for ‘Sustainable Living Brands’, measured against the rest of the company’s portfolio.

### 50%

Reduction in greenhouse gas emissions from manufacturing since 2008, minimizing its carbon footprint.

### 1 billion

People engaged on the topic of health and well-being, underscoring the business’s commitment to improving consumer health through its product offerings.

### Sustainable business practices and circular economy initiatives

### Sustainable business practices and circular economy initiatives

### Challenge:

Patagonia wanted to address environmental concerns associated with the apparel industry, including waste and resource depletion, while maintaining profitability and brand loyalty.

### Solution:

The company implemented a circular economy model through initiatives like the ‘Worn Wear’ program, which encourages customers to buy used Patagonia gear and recycle their old items.

### 25%

Increase in sales attributed to the ‘Worn Wear’ program.

### 1 million

Pounds of clothing diverted from landfills through recycling initiatives.

### 70%

Customer retention rate among ‘Worn Wear’ participants, taking the company’s brand loyalty to new heights.
